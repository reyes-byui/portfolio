WHAT I DO

Web building and designing skills were developed through coursework at BYU-Idaho, focusing on HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. This education provided a solid foundation in creating and styling web pages, along with adding interactive elements to enhance user experience. Additionally, I designed the first business page of La Vogue using WordPress, further honing practical web development and design skills. My passion and dedication are all about aligning with your objectives, making your goals my priority.

Filming skills were honed through practical experience combined with a passion for continuous education. Utilizing Adobe Creative Cloud applications for video editing, I have developed a keen eye for detail and a strong ability to bring creative visions to life. This blend of hands-on practice and dedication to learning ensures high-quality, polished results in every project.

Photography has transitioned from a mere hobby to a significant facet of my endeavors, where I dedicate my efforts to capturing and immortalizing endless moments. This art form allows me to focus on unique perspectives and ideas, enabling me to highlight aspects of life that often evade casual observation. Through my lens, I aim to convey stories, emotions, and the beauty found in everyday scenes, transforming my passion into a meaningful pursuit that continually inspires and challenges me.

Through continuous education, I have enhanced my practical experience in social media advertising. From gaining insights into key performance indicators to effectively setting and achieving targets, I am well-equipped to assist you with creating digital content and developing impactful advertisements. Let me leverage my skills and knowledge to help you reach your audience and achieve your marketing goals effectively.

Strategic planning of how your audience perceives your brand is as crucial as determining where you want your brand positioned within your target market. With my comprehensive knowledge, skills, experience, and commitment to continuous education, I am dedicated to helping you establish a robust brand identity. This identity will not only foster customer loyalty but also drive sustained success in the competitive market landscape. Together, let's craft a brand narrative that resonates deeply with your audience and sets your business apart.
