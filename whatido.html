WHAT I DO

Web building and design skills were developed through coursework at BYU-Idaho, focusing on HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. This education provided a solid foundation in creating and styling web pages while incorporating interactive elements to enhance user experience. Practical experience was further refined by designing the first business page of La Vogue using WordPress, strengthening expertise in web development and design. Passion and dedication are centered on aligning with client objectives, ensuring that every project is tailored to meet specific goals and priorities.

Specializing in digital media, this work combines practical experience with a passion for continuous education. Expertise spans content creation, web development, social media management, and graphic design. Utilizing Adobe Creative Cloud for video editing ensures a keen eye for detail and the ability to bring creative visions to life. Responsive websites are crafted using WordPress, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, while social media strategy, engagement, and analytics are effectively managed. Skills in branding, print design, and digital marketing contribute to polished, high-quality results. This blend of hands-on practice and continuous learning ensures impactful digital solutions.

With a strong foundation in digital marketing and advertising, this work blends strategic thinking with creativity to drive brand visibility and engagement. Expertise covers SEO, content marketing, social media management, and paid advertising, ensuring data-driven campaigns that resonate with target audiences. Proficiency in Adobe Creative Cloud enhances video editing and graphic design, creating compelling visuals that strengthen brand messaging. A deep understanding of affiliate marketing, email campaigns, and analytics allows for optimized performance and effective monetization strategies. Through continuous learning and hands-on experience, every project is crafted to deliver measurable, high-impact results.

Expertise in social media management ensures strategic content creation, audience engagement, and brand growth across multiple platforms. A data-driven approach optimizes content strategy, scheduling, analytics, and ad campaigns to maximize reach and interaction. Community building, trend analysis, and hashtag strategy are integrated to maintain relevance and drive organic growth. By combining creativity with performance-driven insights, social media presence is strengthened to deliver impactful, measurable results.

Strategic planning of how your audience perceives your brand is as crucial as determining where you want your brand positioned within your target market. With my comprehensive knowledge, skills, experience, and commitment to continuous education, I am dedicated to helping you establish a robust brand identity. This identity will not only foster customer loyalty but also drive sustained success in the competitive market landscape.
